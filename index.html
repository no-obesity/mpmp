THE STORY OF THE BIKING BOY IN BLUE

Morgan Parry: Moped Police is a series following the misadventures of the titular character as he solves petty crimes in the fictional village of Griffithstown while riding on his moped. In the pilot episode, our car copper wants to prove to his family he can be a “real” police officer, but be careful what you wish for Morgan! He is hilariously underprepared when his mother gets kidnapped. Can he save the day?

New episodes every Friday, released on Netflix and Youtube
